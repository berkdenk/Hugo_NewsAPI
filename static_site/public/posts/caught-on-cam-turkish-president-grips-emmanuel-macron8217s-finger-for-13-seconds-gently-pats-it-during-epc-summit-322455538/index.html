Macron Appears Uncomfortable as Erdogan Holds His Finger The exchange began innocently with a handshake, but when Macron reached out with his other hand, Erdogan, while still seated, grasped Macron’s index finger and continued speaking. The video has led to a flurry of speculation online, with many interpreting Erdogan’s gesture as a subtle assertion of dominance. In response, Erdogan reportedly reacted by firmly holding Macron’s finger, signaling his refusal to be outmaneuvered in body language.
