However, instead of releasing Macron’s hand after the handshake, the Turkish President unexpectedly grasped the French leader’s finger and held on to it, while he appeared to stare off into the distance. The next few seconds appear awkward for Macron, who stood there talking and trying to free his hand. Erdogan released Macron’s finger a few seconds later, with both leaders laughing shortly afterwards.
